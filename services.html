Seamless Shopping & Reliable Services

Enjoy a smooth shopping experience with easy browsing, secure payment options, and timely delivery. We ensure your fruits arrive fresh, with a smooth process from start to finish.

Add to Cart / Buy Now

Shopping for fresh fruits is simple and quick! Browse our selection and click "Add to Cart" for items you want to purchase. If you're ready to complete your purchase right away, you can click the "Buy Now" button to skip the cart and proceed directly to checkout. You can review and modify your cart at any time before finalizing your order.

Delivery Time

Our dedicated delivery team operates from 9 AM to 6 PM, seven days a week. We strive to deliver your fruits as quickly as possible, ensuring they arrive fresh and in perfect condition. You can expect your order to reach you within the same day or the next, depending on your location and the time the order is placed.

How to Pay

Making a payment is simple! Once you're ready to check out, visit the payment section on our website. You can choose from various secure payment methods. For instance, you can use PhonePe by visiting www.phonepe.com, or select any other payment platform like Google Pay or Paytm directly on our checkout page. Rest assured, all payments are processed safely and securely.

Payment Options

To make your shopping experience smooth and hassle-free, we offer a wide range of secure payment options. You can complete your purchase using popular online payment platforms such as PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, or via Credit/Debit Cards. We ensure that all transactions are processed securely, so you can shop with peace of mind.

Delivery Charge

We charge a nominal delivery fee of Rs. 200 for orders underRs. 500 However, if your order exceeds Rs. 500, we offer free delivery to ensure that you get your fresh fruits without worrying about additional charges. Our goal is to make your shopping experience as convenient and affordable as possible.
